About us

Joyixir is an indie game studio that develops fun and entertaining free-to-play games on mobile platforms that engages thousands of people.

We are a small team but with big ambitions. Recently, we founded our headquarter in Tartu, Estonia. Currently, our focus is on hyper-casual games working with the biggest publishers in the industry. Our long-term plan is to focus on casual and mid-core games which keep people entertained for years.

Other than being a video game company, we are a startup company with entrepreneurial mindset and thirst for innovation from our products to our company culture. While developing games, we think about innovative game design principles that leverages machine learning and data-intensive approaches to forge personalized and engaging games with mindfulness.
